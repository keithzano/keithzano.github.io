About us

Our Company

Rehmsplastis a private limited manufacturing company formed in June in 2020 to manufacture plastic bricks as well as reduce waste in a profitable manner. The company specializes in recycling plastic into plastic bricks. We have a highly committed team possessing extensive breath of experience across design and manufacturing. We are currently located at 357 Melfort. Our premises of modern workshops are equipped with Precious Plastic machinery to help us with the manufacturing process.

To commence with, we are targeting companies and individuals who are in construction industry such asindividuals who are building their houses and companies who uses plastic as raw materials such as MegaPak.

Vision statement

  • To produce cost-effective plastic bricks and cover 1/5 of houses built in Zimbabwe by 2030.

Mission statement

  • To position the company as a society and environmentally friendly organization while still remaining profitable as a business.

Value

  • Integrity
  • Professional
  • Innovation
  • Reliability
  • Accountability

Objectives

  • To generate a profit after tax of more than $10000 by October 2022.
  • To recycle a minimum of 5 tonnes of waste plastics and thus producing minimum of 10 000 bricksby October 2021.
